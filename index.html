How to use?

Press or hold the buttons "+" or "-" to select the start time.
Or you can insert time in the form below.

Press the Start button. The second click on this button stops the contdown.

The Reset button returns timer to 00:00.

During the countdown, the time control is locked.

In any time you can collapse this panel by clicking on the button in the center.
